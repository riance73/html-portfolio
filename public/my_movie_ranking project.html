Top Movies of All Time according to Jennifer Dorcy

My Best 3 Movies

The Stand

This is my favorite movie because it sticks to the book. It is very engaging.

PS I Love You

This is a heartbreaking movie. I love it because you really connect to the characters. It is one of the only movies that has ever made me cry.

Fifth Element

This is one my favorite movies because it pulls you in right from the start. There is action, paranormal, mystery, and drama.
